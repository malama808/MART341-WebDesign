Naupaka

The story of Naupaka is a legend story of why a beautiful native shrub is both found in the mountain and at the shoreline beaches. Blooming from this shrub is only half a blossom. One half blooms at the beach shrub and the other half blooms is in the mountain. This love story speaks on, love, jealousy, and seperation. Hula practioners songwriters and storytellers are those inspired by this story.
