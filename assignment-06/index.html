  • 1 Bag Gummy Bears

  • 1 Bag Gummy Worms
  • 1 1/2 oz. Lemon Peel Strips
  • 2 tsp. Li Hing Mui Powder
  • 4 tbsp. Lemon Juice

directions

  1. Open all the Gummy Bags
  2. Put all the Gummies in one mixing bowl
  3. Add the 4 tbsp. Lemon Juice to the Gummies and mix the juice until all gummies are saturated
  4. Add the 2 tspn. of Li Hing Powder to the Gummies and mix well until all gummies are saturated
  5. Add the Lemon Peels Strips and mix it all together
  6. Allow all Ingredients to sit for one hour and periodically mix
  7. Ready to eat!
