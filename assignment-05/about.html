Gabrielle Ahuliʻi is an author and librarian that has written a series of Hawaiian stories and tales. Ahuliʻi recently published a series of legend adaptations for children ages 0-4. Ahuliʻi recieved her Masterʻs in Library & Information Science, from University of Hawaii in 2016. Ahuliʻi also recieved her bachelorʻs of Fine Arts, Theatre Design & Production, 2013.
